<h4><strong>Marina Bay Sands Expo &amp; Convention Centre</strong></h4>
<p>Set against the pulse of Singapore’s business district, the Marina Bay
Sands Expo &amp; Convention Centre offers the perfect stage for the SCRI
Clinical Trials Symposium 2026. Besides possessing world-class infrastructure,
Marina Bay Sands stands out for its unwavering commitment to sustainability
— the venue is LEED Platinum certified (making it the first MICE venue
in Asia Pacific to do so), ISO 20121 certified for sustainable event management
and holds the Singapore MICE Sustainability Certification at the Gold Tier.</p>
<p>At Marina Bay Sands, we are not just gathering in Singapore’s most iconic
space — we are doing so in a location that exemplifies innovation and environmental
leadership.</p>
