<h4><strong>SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026</strong></h4>

![](/images/HeroBanner2025.jpg)

The SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026 is back - bolder, bigger, and more future-focused than ever. Set in the heart of Singapore’s thriving research ecosystem, this flagship event brings together the brightest minds in global clinical research for two power-packed days of visionary thinking and transformative collaboration.

Anchored by the theme “Clinical Trials Reimagined: Advancing Innovation, Inclusion, and Impact,” the Symposium spotlights the next era of clinical excellence - one where smarter technologies, inclusive trial design, and global partnerships converge to deliver what truly matters: better outcomes for patients worldwide.

Expect dynamic thought-leadership keynotes, high-level panel discussions, and deep-dive sessions led by influential clinicians, investigators, policymakers, and industry trailblazers. This is where the sector’s most pressing challenges meet its most groundbreaking solutions - from leveraging AI and real-world data, to boosting trial efficiency, to empowering patient voices like never before.

![](/images/SCRIDAY1_360.jpg)

Beyond the exchange of ideas, the Symposium serves as a catalyst for progress - strengthening collaboration across Asia and beyond, sparking innovation that scales, and paving the way for clinical trials that are more agile, more accessible, and more impactful.

With Singapore firmly established as a global leader in clinical research, SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026 is the place to redefine what the future of trials should look like - and the role we all play in shaping it.

Join us as we drive the breakthroughs that will transform healthcare for generations to come.
