<h4><strong>SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/HeroBanner2025.jpg">
</div>
<p>The SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026 is back - bolder, bigger, and
more future-focused than ever. Set in the heart of Singapore’s thriving
research ecosystem, this flagship event brings together the brightest minds
in global clinical research for two power-packed days of visionary thinking
and transformative collaboration.</p>
<p>Anchored by the theme “Clinical Trials Reimagined: Advancing Innovation,
Inclusion, and Impact,” the Symposium spotlights the next era of clinical
excellence - one where smarter technologies, inclusive trial design, and
global partnerships converge to deliver what truly matters: better outcomes
for patients worldwide.</p>
<p>Expect dynamic thought-leadership keynotes, high-level panel discussions,
and deep-dive sessions led by influential clinicians, investigators, policymakers,
and industry trailblazers. This is where the sector’s most pressing challenges
meet its most groundbreaking solutions - from leveraging AI and real-world
data, to boosting trial efficiency, to empowering patient voices like never
before.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/SCRIDAY1_360.jpg">
</div>
<p>Beyond the exchange of ideas, the Symposium serves as a catalyst for progress
- strengthening collaboration across Asia and beyond, sparking innovation
that scales, and paving the way for clinical trials that are more agile,
more accessible, and more impactful.</p>
<p>With Singapore firmly established as a global leader in clinical research,
SCRI Clinical Trials Symposium 2026 is the place to redefine what the future
of trials should look like - and the role we all play in shaping it.</p>
<p>Join us as we drive the breakthroughs that will transform healthcare for
generations to come.</p>
