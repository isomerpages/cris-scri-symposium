	<h4><b>Dr Joonwoo Bahn</b></h4>
<b>Professor, Department of Convergence Medicine<br> 
Director, Clinical Research Center, Asan Medical Center, Korea 
</b>
	
<p>Dr Joonwoo Bahn, MD, PhD, is a Professor and Head of the Department of Convergence Medicine at Asan Medical Center, Korea, and serves as Director of the Clinical Research Center / Clinical Trial Center. He has over fourteen years of industry experience at AstraZeneca and Gilead Sciences, where he held leadership roles in Medical Affairs and Clinical Operations across Korea and several Asian countries. Dr. Bahn received his MD and PhD from Seoul National University and completed his clinical training in Internal Medicine and Allergy at Seoul National University Hospital. He previously served as an Assistant Professor specializing in Allergy and Respiratory Medicine at Hallym University Sacred Heart Hospital.
</p>
