<p>Mr Henry Yau is the Group CEO of HKU Clinical Research Group (HongCRES), Managing Director of HKU Clinical Trials Centre (HKU-CTC), Managing Director of Gleneagles Clinical Trials Centre (Gleneagles CTC), Honorary Assistant Professor of Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST) and Non-executive Director of MUST Clinical Trials Centre (MUST-CTC).</p>
<p>
Mr Yau also undertakes different roles in many international bodies such as Steering Board Member and Past Chairperson of the International Clinical Trial Center Network (ICN), Member of the Working Group on Good Governance Practice for Research Institutions of the Council for International Organizations of Medical Sciences (CIOMS), Advisor (Asia) of Training and Resources in Research Ethics Evaluation Program (TRREE), Member of the Clinical Operations Professional Committee of DIA China, and Expert Member of the Academic Advisory Committee of the Greater Bay Area International Clinical Trials Center (BAY TRIAL).
