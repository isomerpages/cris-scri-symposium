	<h4><b>Dr Troy Puar</b></h4>
<b>Head, Senior Consultant, Endocrinology<br>
Changi General Hospital, SingHealth, Singapore
</b>
	
<p>Dr Troy Puar is the current Head and Senior Consultant in the Department of Endocrinology and Adrenal Hypertension Center at Changi General Hospital (CGH). He is also the lead Principal Investigator for the Shimadzu–Changi Clinomics Centre (SC3) which utilises state-of-the-art liquid chromatography tandem mass spectrometry for clinical care of primary aldosteronism.<br>
	
Dr Puar is a recipient of several National Medical Research Council (NMRC) awards, and is currently a nationally-funded clinician scientist with a focus on Primary Aldosteronism. He successfully led a nationwide multicentre clinical trial, making Singapore the first–in–Asia to use functional PET imaging to diagnose patients with unilateral Primary Aldosteronism. Beyond Singapore, Dr Puar is also the inaugural President of the ASEAN Network of Adrenal Hypertension (ANAH), which focuses on improving treatment of Adrenal Hypertension in the region.
</p>
